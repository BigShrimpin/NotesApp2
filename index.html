What is Notelligient?

Notelligient is the premier notetaking service that allows you to sign in, store, and save your digital notes and information to be accessed anywhere you go! Our website is available on all browsers, platforms, and devices, allowing you to access and edit your notes from anywhere in the world.

How did I make this?

I created Notelligient using vanilla JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I used Node.JS, a runtime environment, to help connect my backend, Firebase, to my JavaScript. This allowed me to authenticate users through login, and save the data that they enter into the notes in a secure database.

Why did I make this?

I chose to make Notelligient because I always end up forgetting an idea I had come up with, or some sort of task that I needed to do. This app will let other users- alongside myself- to keep track of ideas, spark notes, and other use cases that users find that suit their needs in the moment.
